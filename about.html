About EcoThreads Co.

Tagline: Sustainable Style for a Better Tomorrow.

EcoThreads Co. was founded to reduce textile waste and make sustainable style accessible. We source recycled fibers and partner with small-batch manufacturers who meet strict environmental and labor standards. From fabric selection to packaging, every decision is made to minimize environmental impact while delivering comfortable, timeless apparel.

Our goal is simple: create clothing you’re proud to wear. We believe fashion shouldn’t cost the earth — which is why we focus on durable designs, transparent sourcing, and programs that repurpose unsold fabric into community projects.

Fact Sheet

  • Founded: 2024
  • Headquarters: Portland, OR
  • Materials: Recycled cotton, recycled polyester, organic cotton blends
  • Shipping: Carbon-offset option at checkout
